HOW TO PLAY

WHY UNCHAIRTED?

A PERSONAL STORY 👋

I'm Vitalii, and I've spent years working as a Computer Vision engineer. After 12-hour coding marathons in my chair, I could practically hear my spine begging for mercy. I needed to act! Otherwise, I was risking a lifetime of being permanently hunched over and howling in pain.

THE FIX 🦾

Stretching felt like a chore, and the gym was too far. So I used my background in AI to turn my webcam into a personal trainer. We don’t collect any data—everything runs 100% locally in your browser. Just hit play and get moving. It works on any device with a camera!

🔒 100% PRIVATE
All processing is local.

YOUR PRIVACY

ZERO DATA COLLECTION 🔒

Your privacy is our priority. UNCHAIRTED runs entirely in your browser. We don't have a backend that stores your images or video.

LOCAL PROCESSING 💻

The camera feed is processed in real-time on your device. No video frames are ever sent to any server or third party. You can even run the game offline once loaded.

🚫 NO TRACKING
No cookies.
📷 NO STORAGE
